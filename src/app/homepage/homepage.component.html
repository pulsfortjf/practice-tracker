I Need To Practice

Hello! Welcome to INTP! Your one-stop solution for efficient musical practice!

You can use the toolbar at the top to navigate between the different pages.

  • Planning
    This is a weekly calendar where you can set your practice goals for the week as well as which days you wish to practice.
    After setting a goal and practice days, your practice time for each practice day you have selected for that week will automatically be calculated for you.
  • Song List
    This page will let you listen to the pieces you are currently working on so that you do not have to leave the application.
    You can enter the URL to a recording of the piece on Youtube, and that song will automatically appear on the page for you to listen to.
    If you wish to save your song list, clicking the "Save" button will download a text file that contains all of the URLs you have uploaded.
    If you wish to load a song list, you can enter the URLs of the songs into the textbox by copy/pasting from a previous saved list.
    Then, clicking the "Load" button will load those songs into your Song List. This can also be used to load multiple songs at the same time.
