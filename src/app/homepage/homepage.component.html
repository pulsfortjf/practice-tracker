I Need To Practice

Hello! Welcome to INTP! Your one-stop solution for efficient musical practice!

You can use the toolbar at the top to navigate between the different pages.

  • Planning
    This is a weekly calendar where you can set your practice goals for each day of the week as well as which days you wish to practice.
    After setting a goal and practice days, your total practice time for that week will automatically be calculated for you.
    If you wish to save your plan for the week, click the Save button.
    Note: Always remember to save again after making any changes to your weekly plan!
    If you wish to start over, clicking the Clear button will clear your current saved plan.
  • Song List
    This page will let you listen to the pieces you are currently working on so that you do not have to leave the application.
    You can enter the name of a song followed by a comma and then the URL to a recording of the piece on Youtube, click the Add button, and that song will appear on the page for you to listen to.
    If you wish to save your song list, click the Save button.
    If you wish to remove a song from the song list, enter its URL into the textbox and then click the Delete button.
